1. Introduction

Welcome to Rip360 Fitness Pro ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services. Rip360 Fitness Pro is developed by entrepreneur Tyrone Hayes, founder of RIPPED CITY INC and Digesting Life Balance nonprofit.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile information (age, height, weight, fitness goals)
  • Account credentials and preferences
  • Payment information (processed by secure third-party processors)

2.2 Health and Fitness Data

  • Workout routines, exercise logs, and progress tracking
  • Nutrition data, meal plans, and calorie intake
  • Body measurements and transformation photos
  • Health metrics and bloodwork analysis (when provided)
  • Medical information and supplement usage (optional)

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • Usage data (features used, time spent, app interactions)
  • Location data (when enabled for gym check-ins or nearby services)
  • Camera and photo library access (for progress photos and barcode scanning)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalized Experience: Create customized workout plans, meal recommendations, and coaching
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your fitness journey and provide analytics
  • AI-Powered Features: Generate workout routines, analyze bloodwork, and provide health insights
  • Coaching Services: Connect you with certified coaches and trainers
  • Product Recommendations: Suggest supplements and fitness products from our shop
  • Community Features: Enable sharing and social interactions (with your consent)
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and provide technical assistance
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and functionality

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Limited Sharing

  • Coaches and Trainers: Fitness data shared with assigned coaches (with your consent)
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist in app functionality (payment processing, cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales (with notice)

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with established providers

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Export: Download your fitness and health data

6.2 Communication Preferences

  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Manage push notification settings
  • Control social sharing and community features

6.3 Location and Camera Permissions

  • Disable location services in app settings
  • Revoke camera and photo library access
  • Manage app permissions through device settings
